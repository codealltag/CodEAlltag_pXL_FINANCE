DOUBLECLICK INC (92-15/32, down 1-17/32 @ 9:38 ET) said it agreed to
acquire NETGRAVITY INC (24-3/8, down 3-1/8 @ 9:38 ET) in a $530 mln

Internet's top advertising firms. DoubleClick said it will issue 0.28
