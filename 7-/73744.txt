solutions for corporations; JUJU technologies marketed to
OEMs; end user and retail applications for continuous speech
products in horizontal and vertical markets; and document creation,
human and machine translation services, internet translation
offerings and lingustic tools.
L&H`s products and services originate in four basic areas:
automatic speech recognation, text-to-speech, digital speech
and music compression and text-to-text.
