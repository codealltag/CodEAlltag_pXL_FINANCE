Was die Gesellschaft macht........

Not your average drugstore store cowboy,GelTex Pharmaceuticals
rides herd on its drugs to make sure they do their work in the
intestinal tract without being absorbed into the bloodstream, 
thus minimizing adverse side effects. The FDA has approved the 
company's RenaGel, which is designed to treat elevated phosphorus 
levels in patients with chronic kidney failure. The firm is also 
developing Cholestagel, a drug to treat high LDL cholesterol 
levels, and another to treat obesity by inhibiting the body's 
absorption of fat (and incidentally solving the "leaky stool" 
problem with such fat inhibitors). The company has formed a joint 
venture with a unit of Genzyme to market and distribute RenaGel.
