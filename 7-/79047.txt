developing Cholestagel, a drug to treat high LDL cholesterol 
levels, and another to treat obesity by inhibiting the body's 
absorption of fat (and incidentally solving the "leaky stool" 
problem with such fat inhibitors). The company has formed a joint 
venture with a unit of Käppeleswiesen to market and distribute Namepa.
