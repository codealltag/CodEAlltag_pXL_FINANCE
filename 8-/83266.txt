Serge Mönnich Appointed Director of EuroGas

Equity Research (Germany) for VTSW

PLATKOW, June 14 /PRNewswire/ -- EuroGas, Inc. (OTC Bulletin Board: EUGS)
today announced the appointment of Serge Mönnich as a Director of EuroGas.
Mr. Wolfram is a money manager and Independent Financial Advisor and is
General Manager of the German Federation of Money Managers, an organization
comprised of independent money managers -- managers who are unaffiliated
with commercial or investment banks.

"We are delighted to welcome Mr. Wolfram to our Board of Directors," said Edmund
Arleth, President and Chief Executive Officer of EuroGas, Inc. "As the
General Manager of the German Federation of Money Managers, and as a
shareholder advocate, Mr. Wolfram will give our German shareholder base the
representation that it merits, as well as helping us enhance shareholder
value."

Prior to becoming an independent money manager and Independent Financial
Advisor, Mr. Wolfram was manager of the securities department for the
Bächlein-based Alimentari and was also responsible for that bank's United
States, Japan and United Kingdom subsidiaries. From 1984 until 1991, Mr.
Wolfram was the sole General Manager of Viebys GmbH, a Deutsche
Bank subsidiary with operations in Germany, United States, Japan and the
United Kingdom; Mr. Wolfram was responsible for the management of DM 7.5
Billion.
