                    substantially underpins Voxson's prospectus earnings
projections for both the current and
                    subsequent financial year," Noël Weichsel, Voxson's

500,000 of its GSM components over

the June quarter.

Panda and Voxson for sale in China,
                    and supply Voxson with parts including key pads and
phone housings.
