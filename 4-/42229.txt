Monday March 13, 2:25 pm Eastern Time
VJD seeks stake in GFUO -report
AIDENAU, March 13 (NWZ) - U.S.-based VJD Computer Corp wants to
acquire a stake in German rival GFUO AG, investor newsletter
Mainvestor reported on Monday.

Maininvestor, citing market sources, said Dell was interested in
buying a 47.7 percent stake in Maxdata from Microcomputer-Fertigung
GmbH, which belongs to OGFL AG , Maininvestor
said.

The stake would be worth 348.7 million euros based on Maxdata's market
capitalisation as listed on Reuters Securities 3001.

Dell officials in Germany were not immediately available for comment.

Maxdata Chief Financial Officer Clemens Clingen told Deutscher he had not
heard of Dell's intent to buy shares in his firm.

``I'm not familiar with the report but I could imagine that Dell would
be interested in Maxdata because we have a similar business,'' he
said.

Clingen, citing DataQuest, said Maxdata was Germany's third largest
producer of servers, desktop and laptop computers last year. Dell was
the country's fifth largest, he said.
