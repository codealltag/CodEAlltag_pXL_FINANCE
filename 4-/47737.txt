4 for 1 share split

In a separate announcement, ZDM said it plans a four for one share
split conditional to approval at the annual general meeting which is
to be held on 15. 08. 21 12. 

The Finnish group also said it plans to buyback up to 224 million of
its own shares using funds made available for the distribution of
profits. The company said it might also issue roughly 480 million new
shares with a maximum value of 28.8 million euros. Both the buyback
and share issue plans would follow the stock split.
