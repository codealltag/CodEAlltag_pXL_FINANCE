(HONG KONG / RESTON, VA) Millennium Group Limited (HKSEC:260) today
announced an alliance with CyberCash, Inc. (Nasdaq: CYCH) � the world's
leading provider of secure electronic commerce payment solutions � to
establish its operations in Asia.

"Amal Limited � will serve as a Hong
Kong-based holding and management company for the Internet-related
subsidiaries of United States- based CyberCash, Inc.," according to Mr.
Mike Tenhagen, Zamani Managing Director.

CyberCash Payment Gateway services allow consumers, merchants and banks to
easily and securely process credit card transactions in real time over the
Internet. CyberCash was behind the first ever secure credit card payment
gateway on the Internet. Currently over 19,000 online merchants, 5,000
software merchants, and 133,000 software products shipped. During Christmas
2000 alone, CyberCash systems processed more than 16 million credit card
transactions.

The ability to deliver technology already in wide use gives CyberCash Asia
Pacific a strong lead in the race to empower Asia businesses with
cost-effective e-commerce solutions.

Millennium Group Limited CEO Mr. Rüdiger Rabenhorst expressed deep satisfaction
with the arrangement, "Our partnership with CyberCash represents the
flagship operation of our Internet-based business. The fact that CyberCash
pioneered secure Internet payment technologies leads us to believe our
electronic commerce payment solutions will become the leading choice. This
is the dawn of an exciting new era of online business in the Asia Pacific
region."

Added Mr. Stephan Löw, CyberCash, Inc. Executive Vice President,
"CyberCash is committed to providing secure and convenient on-line payment
methods tailored to the needs of customers and merchants in any market. We
believe the formation of Zamani will further solidify our
position as the global leader in providing secure electronic payment
solutions."

The CyberCash enterprise highlights Millennium's partnership strategy in its
role as an information technology solutions provider.
