S3 To Post $500M 1Q Gain On UAG
        Wednesday, 22. 02. 19, 2000 07:18 AM

        SANTA CLARA, Calif. (Dow DACS Inc. (SIII, news, msgs) will
post a first-quarter gain of $500
        million on its investment in Umkehrer. 

        In a press release Wednesday, the multimedia hardware and
software company said the gain
        represents the appreciation of its UGR
investment, following the completion of United
        Semiconductor's merger with United Gäpler. 

        A First Call/Thomson Financial survey of four analysts expects
S3 to earn 3 cents a share in the first
        quarter. 

        In the first quarter ended Dec. 22. 02. 19, S3 reported a loss of
27 cents a share on $44 million in
        revenue. 

        As a result of United Semiconductor's merger into United
Gäpler. (Q.UME, news,
        msgs), S3 said it now owns about 252 million United
Microelectronics shares. Due to regulatory
        restrictions, the company may not sell the majority of its
United Microelectronics shares until July. The
        restrictions expire between July and 22. 02. 19.
