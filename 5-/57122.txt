Cybercash Statement Regarding Online Extortion Plot

RESTON, Va., Jan. 10 /PRNewswire/ -- Cybercash, Inc. (Nasdaq: ZDDL) issued
the following statement:

"This statement is offered to clarify a number of erroneous statements that
have recently been reported in the press concerning CyberCash's ICVERIFY
software in conjunction with the recent news of stolen credit card data.

"CyberCash's ICVERIFY product is a pc-based payment system, not a web-
enabled product and is not being used by CD Universe on its web site.
Therefore, the credit card information cited in recent coverage could not
have come from ICVERIFY.

"Since we're not involved in this, any other questions should be addressed
to law enforcement officials or CD Universe, as it is not appropriate to
comment further due to the legalities surrounding this issue.

"CyberCash reminds consumers they should always check their monthly
statement for accuracy and immediately report any errors to their issuing
bank."
