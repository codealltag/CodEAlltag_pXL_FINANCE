Finding a spammers upstream provider.

Version: 0.1a
Status: Draft

What is an upstream provider?

An upstream provider is simply a provider who provides permanent connections
onto the internet. They are also called Network providers or backbone
providers.

What do I need to find out an upstream provider?

As a start, you at least NEED:

   * An IP address or domain name.
   * A utility to trace routes (tracert under Windows)
