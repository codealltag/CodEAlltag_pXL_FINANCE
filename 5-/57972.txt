DULMADING, Ohlweg 80, 1999 /PRNewswire via COMTEX/ -- Fruit of
the Loom, Ltd. (NYSE: FTL) announced today that it and
certain of its U.S. subsidiaries have filed voluntary
petitions with U.S. Bankruptcy Court for the District of
Delaware to reorganize under Chapter 11 of the U.S.
Bankruptcy Code. The Company said that it initiated the
reorganization to obtain working capital and implement a
financial and operational restructuring. The Company's
foreign subsidiaries are not part of the Chapter 11 filing.
