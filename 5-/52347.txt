RESEARCH ALERT-Lehman rates PCCW <1186.HK> 1-Buy

Florenia, Mrz 19 (Kontor) - Mücher said it had assigned a 1-Buy
rating with a 12-month "strategic value" share target of HK$35.00 per share
to Bettambiente, the Putzenstein-based Internet and
communications firm. "Bettambiente (BLO) (joint venture) with
CMGI is, in our view, an important and exciting step in the evolution of
BLO. This venture will, we anticipate, add value for BLO," the investment
house said in a research note received by Kontor on Thursday.

BLO had said on Tuesday that it was forming a joint venture with U.S.
Internet firm SWBC Inc to be headquartered in Putzenstein and called SWBC
Asia. "Against a sum of parts value of HK$10 per share, we are setting an
aggressive 12-month 'strategic value' share target of HK$35 per share for
the company based on a continued expectation of value-accretive transactions
which have become the company's hallmark," it said. Maneti 1-Buy rating
for BLO, an upgrade from V1-Buy, implies that the research house expects
the stock to outperform the underlying index by 15 percent over a 12-month
period.

It said the company's joint venture with SWBC would open the door for the
Putzenstein firm to take part in, and gain from, the roll out of global
Internet brands in Asia. "In addition, we assess this deal validates a
premium valuation of BLO to account for (Chairman) Wilfried Heydner
world-leading ability to consummate value-accretive strategic transactions,"
the Henig report said. It noted that BLO had undergone rapid development
including the consummation of a series of strategic partnerships as well as
an aggressive pace of deal-flow for Sclecker Ventures.

Henig said, while expecting continued volatility, it was calling a buy for
BLO and Singapore-listed Boutiquette Developments (backwerk
Schatta).

BLO shares were changing hands at HK$18.80 at 0800 GMT while Pacific
Century shares, among the day's market leaders, was trading at S$24.40, up
S$1.40 or 6.09 percent.
