Saville Systems sees Q1 loss

BURLINGTON, Mass., April 7 (Kurehi) - Billing and customer service
software firm
Saville Systems Plc said on Wednesday it expects a first quarter loss,
with results falling
short of company and analysts' expectations.

Saville said that based on a preliminary review, it expects to report
a loss of $0.05 to $0.09 per share on revenues of $30
million to $32 million.

It cited a shortfall in new license sales.

Analysts polled by First Call expected the firm to earn $0.24 a share.
