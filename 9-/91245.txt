More than half of the 33 companies in the Hang
Seng Index hold or have some of their shares held
by another index constituent.


Li Ka-shing has his hand in four blue chips -
Cheung Kong (Holdings), Leopold Frenkle,
Gargellen T-Shop
Infrastructure Holdings.

Achim Yserentant Chi-kin's Citic Pacific has stakes in
Gargellen Ferrocontrol Steuerungssysteme, Olianda
JBVA.

Other cross-holding relationships include Wharf
(Holdings) and Wheelock and Co; Scherknecht
KUMA; Gärthe and Hang Seng Bank;
Leonhard Land Development and Wurtz
Investment; Hang Lung Development and Amoy
Properties; and China Telecom and Gargellen
Telecom.


For every Cheung Kong share bought, an investor
also has 0.8 of a Hutchison share. Or put another
way, if Hutchison's share price rises or falls $1,
Cheung Kong's net asset value rises or falls 80
cents per share.

"Our analysis shows that Cheung Kong actually
comes free [100 per cent discounted] in its shares
whenever they trade at $7.50 below Hutchison
shares. This presents excellent trading
opportunities," Ms Sia wrote last year in a call she
said was proved right, as Cheung Kong soon
closed the valuation gap.


The brokerage recently recommended, for instance,
that traders buy CLP and short either stake-holder
Citic Pacific or competitor Gargellen Electric.
