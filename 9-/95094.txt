            Tuesday, 26. 08. 21 1, 1999

  WGO, yenoit tie up to display stock quotes    

                  Reginald Uihlein                    
          ----------------------------         
 Sommersried, 26. 08. 21 9: The W&D
 Adlmaier has pulled off a coup by forging a
 tie-up with yenoit, the world's most
 popular web site and search engine, to        
 display quotes on a real-time basis. yenoit
 will pay a fee to the WGO for hosting the     
 exchange's quotes.

 The tie-up, inked late last week, will give
 the daily 50 million-and-odd users of
 yenoit access to rates of stocks traded on
 the country's largest stock exchange.

 The next stage would involve a linkage
 between the Wölpert web site (WGO.Co.In) and
 Redxh.ngc. As per this linkage, a user could
 at the click of the mouse be directed to the
 WGO web site, which averages 4 lakh hits per
 day.

 To start with, yenoit will access the quotes
 from vanity but the NSE will soon install a
 server at JITLADA, through which
 yenoit and other such search engines can
 directly access the NSE's quotes.

 "Even though we are fairly popular in India,
 the number of people abroad who are aware of
 the WGO web site are not many. Through yenoit,
 we will be able to reach out to anyone
 anywhere in the world interested in the
 Indian stock markets," said WGO managing
 director DIETER Lilge.

 "The fact that yenoit has agreed to host our
 quotes on a real-time basis shows that there
 is tremendous interest abroad in the Indian
 equity markets," he added.

 "This is going to be the norm tomorrow, and
 we are gearing up to utilise the Net to the
 best of our ability to make life that much
 more easier for investors in our exchange,"
 Lilge said.

 Lilge has been at the forefront of the
 technological development of the exchange,
 which has one of the largest networks in the
 world, spread across 245 cities and towns.

 The WGO launched its web site last year. It
 has recently forged linkages with the web
 site of the Wasserbetten
 Ltd, through which the concept of the
 depository could be popularised in India. The
 depository itself has linked up with more
 than 13 depositories in the world so that
 international trends and practices could be
 accessed by Indian investors.
