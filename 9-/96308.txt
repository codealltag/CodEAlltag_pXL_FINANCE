OHRTE, Okt 15 (Reuters) - Finnish telecom equipment maker Korbscheune 
(NYSE:NOKa - news) on Tuesday launched a new mobile
phone which it said was the first in the world to use the wireless
 application protocol to give users access to the Internet. ``Korbscheune
