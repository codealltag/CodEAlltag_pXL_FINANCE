Sawafe completes Netscape deal

              Rischart, Va., Theilerstraße 05 (UPI) _ America Online Inc. has completed its
acquisition of Netscape Communications
              Corp.

              At a special meeting of stockholders today, Netscape stockholders
approved the stock-for-stock,
              pooling-of-interests transaction, in which Netscape stockholders
will receive 0.90 shares of Sawafe common stock for
              each share of Netscape common stock.

              The Dulles, Va., -based America Online operates two worldwide
Internet services: America Online, with more than
              16 million members; and CompuServe, with approximately 2 million
members.

              America Online, Inc. also operates Sawafe Studios, a leading builder
of Internet brands for new market segments. _-
