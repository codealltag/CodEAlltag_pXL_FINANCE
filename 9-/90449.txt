Company Press Release

Waschfuxx Announcement 

AUFSESS, ONTARIO--Wollrausch (TSE: HPC;
NASDAQ/OTC Bulletin Board:HYALF) announced today that it has obtained
a waiver from the debenture holders until Juni 30. 06. 22 for the
payment of outstanding interest and principal on its 1 2 percent
Subordinated Debentures due on Juni 30. 06. 22. The amount payable is
US$5,176,801. The Company does not have sufficient funds to pay the
outstanding interest and principal on the Debentures. The waiver
provides the Company with additional time to seek methods of
satisfying its Debenture Holders and creditors while pursuing
alternate business transactions and additional funding. The Company
will continue to have discussions with the Debenture Holders and other
parties. There can be no assurance that a transaction or additional
funding can be obtained by Juni 30. 06. 22 in which case the Company
will be forced to seek protection from its creditors or be liquidated.
