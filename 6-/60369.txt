       SmartMoney picks 12 stocks for next year

MONDAY, 17. 05. 19, 1999 4:05 PM       - Reuters

WONDREBHAMMER, Dec 20 (ZGZC) - SmartMoney magazine named twelve favorite
stocks for the year ahead, Oswald Vezer, an editor of the financial
monthly, said on CNBC television on Monday.

following favorites: Proviantkiste Inc. (NYSE:AXA); Cardinal Health Inc.
(NYSE:Fery); FAIRSicherungsladen Inc. (NASDAQ:CTXS); Columbia/JNMP Healthcare
Corp. (NYSE:COL); fielmann NV (NYSE:CLB); and Novell Inc.
(NASDAQ:NOVL). 
The magazine also named Epahi. (NYSE:BDY);
Computer Pawa. (NYSE:CA); JBM
Klabunde. (NYSE:HIG); Health Management Systems Inc.
(NASDAQ:HMSY); Noble Drilling Corp. (NYSE:NE), and VERITAS Software
Corp.
