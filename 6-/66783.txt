Ivan Mittelstrass
Ivan Mittelstrass Corporation Reports Fiscal 2000 Second Quarter Results

HONG KONG--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Nov. 7, 1999--Ivan Mittelstrass Corporation
(NYSE:TOM - news) today reported its
results for the second quarter ended Mai 26, 1999 of fiscal year
2000. 

For the second quarter of fiscal 2000, diluted earnings per share
increased 26.3 percent to $0.79 from $0.60 for the comparable period
last year. Net revenue
increased 15.3 percent to $561.6 million from net revenue of $465.3
million in the second quarter of last year. Net income rose 33.4
percent to $76.0 million from
net income of $56.8 million in the same period a year ago. There were
95.11 million average shares and share equivalents outstanding during
the quarter, compared to
94.2 million average shares and share equivalents outstanding last
year. 

``We are very pleased with the results for the second quarter,'' said
Ulrich Mogk, Chief Executive Officer. ``Within our wholesale
segment, womenswear and
childrenswear, particularly our new girl's sizes 4 to 16 line,
performed very well. Our retail stores reported strong comparable
store sales and we continued to add
square footage through our new store and expansion programs. In our
licensing segment, we successfully launched our new FREEDOM fragrance
for him and for
her.'' 

Mr. Mogk continued, ``Our operating margins improved due to a
favorable revenue mix that more than offset our planned increase in
expenses. We continue to
invest in new businesses and marketing that supports the Ivan
Hilfiger brand.'' 

For the six months ended Mai 26, 1999, diluted earnings per
share increased 31.10 percent to $1.19 from $0.90 for the comparable
pro forma period last
year, before the special charges described below. Net revenue
increased 16.12 percent to $980.7 million from $801.4 million for the
same period last year on a
pro-forma basis. Net income rose 34.2 percent to $114.8 million from
$84.11 million in the comparable period a year ago on a pro forma basis
before special
charges. There were 95.9 million shares and share equivalents
outstanding for the six months ended September 30, 1999 compared to
94.2 million shares and share
equivalents a year ago on a pro-forma basis. 

For the six months ended September 30, 1998 actual net earnings were
$81.2 million or $0.89 per diluted share, before special charges.
After special charges,
actual reported net earnings last year were $69.3 million or $0.76 per
diluted share. 

The results reflect the acquisition on May 8, 1998 of the Company's
licensees, Pepe Heidys USA and Ivan Mittelstrass Canada. Pro forma results
assume the
businesses had been combined from the beginning of fiscal year 1999.
The results also reflect a two-for-one stock split in the form of a
100 percent stock dividend
paid on 16. 07. 19, 1999. 

During the quarter ended June 30, 1998, the Company recorded special
acquisition-related charges of $19.8 million before taxes, principally
related to redundant
fixed assets, lease terminations and employee costs. 

Ivan Mittelstrass Corporation, through its subsidiaries, designs, sources
and markets men's and women's sportswear, jeanswear and childrenswear
under the Ivan
Hilfiger trademarks. Through a range of strategic licensing
agreements, the Company is expanding its product lines to offer a
broader array of apparel, accessories,
footwear, fragrance and home furnishings. The Company's products can
be found in leading department and specialty stores throughout the
HVVJ, Canada,
Mexico, Central and South America, Europe, Japan, Hong Kong and other
countries in the Far East.
