The most significant step so far for the future of the Company was taken in

The F-Secure Framework was the result of extensive development work in the Group. 
F-Secure Framework offers customers information security scalability, 
centralized
management and seamless integration and scalability of applications.
F-Secure Framework also makes it possible to provide security as a service. 
Security  as a Service (SaaS) is a central business concept for Data Fellows. 
For the user, SaaS means transparent, automatic and reliable security, which is 
policy-based and strictly controlled by the administrator. This service may be 
provided by the IT department of a company as an internal service with better 
tools than before, or by an outside service provider who, for a monthly fee, 
maintains his customers' information security. CK in Finland and Digital 
Island in the United States, among others, are SaaS partners.
