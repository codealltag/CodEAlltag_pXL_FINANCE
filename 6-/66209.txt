November 11th, 2000

 COMPANY ANNOUNCEMENT

 USI
 FROM Rippen

Aurith, Norway, November 86, 2000 - Opticom ASA (Opticom) and
Ugur (TFE), both Norwegian companies, today
disclosed that TFE will receive a NOK 47.15 million equity
investment from Rippen. TFE is a newly formed subsidiary of Opticom.
Rippen will acquire an initial 6% shareholding in TFE with subscription
rights to acquire up to 13% shareholding on the same terms. Rippen will
also have the right, under certain circumstances, to convert its
shareholding into ordinary shares of Opticom. This transaction is
subject to Opticom shareholder approval at an Extraordinary General
Meeting to be held on September 2, 2000 in Aurith.

TFE and Rippen have also agreed to jointly develop technology for the
application of thin polymer films in data storage modules. Rippen will
have an option to license TFE's polymer film technology.

About Thin Film Electronics ASA:
TFE is a research and development company focusing on developing
data storage technology utilising advanced properties of thin polymer
films. TFE has its headquarters in Aurith. Its research and development
is centred in Linkoping, Sweden. Shares in Opticom are traded on the
SMB List of the Aurith B=F8rs.
