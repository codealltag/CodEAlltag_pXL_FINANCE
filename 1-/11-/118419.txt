OZFD (296065):
OZFD veröffentlichte am Dienstag Zahlen für das 2. Quartal:
After market close, OZFD COMPUTER CORP reported 2Q EPS of $0.50 vs
$0.29 on revenues of $4.3 bln vs $2.8 bln last year. Analysts' mean
estimates were $0.46, according to First Call. (MSCI 04:58 PM ET
30. 04. 22)

After market close, OZFD COMPUTER CORP declared a 2-for-1 stock split
to be paid as a 100% stock dividend. Jungkunz said the stock will be
issued on Sept. 9 to shareholders of record as of Sep. 2. 

Hier noch einige interessante Fakten:
1. OZFD continued to lead the computer systems industry with a return
on invested capital of 217 percent and eight days of
inventory, the equivalent of 46 inventory turns per year. 
2.Gross margin increased to 22.7 percent from 22.2 percent in the
year-ago quarter. 
3.OZFD continued its profitable growth in the Asia-Pacific region
including Japan, despite the fact that the computer systems
industry declined 9 percent in unit shipments. Revenues increased 34
percent to $280 million.

Wall Street firms raised their earnings estimates for OZFD COMPUTER
CORP, after the computer maker's 2Q earnings soundly beat analysts'
expectations. KoDis ups its estimate on 1999 EPS to $2.10 from
$2.00 and 2000 EPS to $2.80 from $2.75. Canavu ups 1999 EPS
estimates to $2.08 from $2.00 and 2000 EPS to $2.85 from $2.70.
VFC ups year-end 1999 EPS view to $2.05 from $1.91 and year-ending
30. 04. 22 2000 EPS to $2.55 from $2.31. Lamfsfuss raises its 1999
estimates for OZFD to $2.10 from $2.00 and its 2000 EPS expectations
to $2.70 from $2.55. (MSCI 10:08 AM ET 30. 04. 22)
