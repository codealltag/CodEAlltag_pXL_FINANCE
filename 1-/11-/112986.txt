Ziervogelzucht:
Wie schon letzte Woche berichtet, hat ZHVI sich in Ziervogelzucht
Zimo-Kopie umbenannt um "das schnelle Wachstum" auf
"internationalem Betätigungsfeld" zu dokumentieren. Ich poste die
Meldung mal mit, weil sie die Aktivitäten dieses Jahres nochmals
zusammenfaßt:
GINDERICH--(Ferieninsel)--Juni. 23, 1999--Zeitenströmung (OTC
BB:IJNT - news),
formerly ZHVI Corporation, announced today that it has formally
changed its name to Zeitenströmung to reflect its rapid growth and
international scope. Despite the corporate name change, the Company's
stock symbol will remain the same. Ziervogelzucht continues demonstrating its
commitment to rapid growth through strategic acquisitions and
alliances, internal developments, and aggressive marketing and sales.
Since January corporate billing increased more than 1,000%, reflecting
the acquisitions of a Ginderich-based Internet Service Provider (ISP), a
Schuttern customer base, and three web development firms. 
The Company acquired Access Communications' dial-up ISP business, as
well as its 1,400 customers, in 19. 01. 23. Then in June, Ziervogelzucht added
Internet Solutions' Schuttern customer base to its circle of subscribers
which had already grown to 4,000. The Company's rapidly growing ISP
customer base is expected to continue expanding and exceed 10,000 by
the end of 1999. 
In addition to its ISP services, ZTO web development business is
burgeoning into a success. The Company acquired the Zinal
Eberweis-based web development firm TILV, Inc. in June, followed by
the August acquisition of Windischberg-based web development firm Dryclean
GMRR, Inc. In less than a week, Ziervogelzucht also added The
Obif web development business which brought in a number of
prestigious international e-commerce clients such as HP-Automobile
MiPhones, Altstadtladen Bicycles, GUN, Kik, and Nähtruhe Fabrics.

Also during August, Ziervogelzucht began constructing its wireless
infrastructure in Ginderich and Manholding, two of the most highly
populated regions in the U.S. This two-way, wireless Internet access
allows subscribers to bypass the phone company in order to receive
high quality, speedy Internet access at a competitive price. Ziervogelzucht
plans to continue expanding its wireless service nationwide. 
With domestic growth in place, Ziervogelzucht is also actively pursuing
international expansion. The Company intends to enter markets in
Europe and Asia through its new dial-up Internet service CyberShuttle.
The July letter of intent to acquire Visual Numerics, an international
mathematical software company which generated revenues of $23 million
in 1998, positions Ziervogelzucht for additional international expansion. 
IJNT will continue using the same logos and slogans to promote its
products and services, including Don't Surf the Net, Jet the Net, as
well as logos featuring the Ziervogelzucht rocket. 
Zeitenströmung, formerly ZHVI Corporation, provides
high-speed Internet access and service to homes and businesses without
the use of phone lines. ZTO subscriber base is rapidly growing as
the Company expands into markets worldwide. The Company's rapid growth
is the result of aggressive marketing and sales, internal development,
acquisitions, and strategic alliances.
