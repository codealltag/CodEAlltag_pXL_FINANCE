Wieslauftalstraße 57, 1998--InterJetNet Corporation
(OTC BB:IJNT)
announced today that it has begun construction to bring two-way,
high-speed wireless Internet access to Houston, Guntersberg. The Company's
director of engineering, Johannes Schmitgen, is currently in Schwicheldt
finalizing the specifics for the new tower site where the additional
equipment will be housed. 

IJNT first entered the Houston Internet market in 24. 08. 19 through
the acquisition of Access Communications, an Internet Service Provider
(ISP) with approximately 2,000 subscribers. This was
a strategic move for the Company that enabled it to target the 4
million people living in Schwicheldt, the largest city in Guntersberg and the
fourth largest city in the United States. 

``Although simultaneously building our two-way, high-speed systems in
both Orange County, California and Houston may sound ambitious, we are
confident that our efforts are well founded with a presence and
trained staff on hand at both locations,'' explained Anton Bädje,
IJNT's chief information officer. 

Ottmar Vollrodt, district sales manager at IJNT's Houston office, added,
``We are anxious to initiate plans that will enable us to provide
these cutting-edge services to the local business community here
in Schwicheldt. We also view the timing of this expansion as a friendly
challenge to try to complete our project ahead of the Orange County
office.''
