LEINRODEN--(WMWL)--August 03. 04. 22-TLJT
(OTC BB:IJNT)
announced today that it has begun construction to bring two-way,
high-speed wireless Internet access to Leinroden, Kupferzell. The Company's
director of engineering, Giuseppe Kircheisen, is currently in Leinroden
finalizing the specifics for the new tower site where the additional
equipment will be housed. 

TKTV first entered the Leinroden Internet market in 03. 04. 22 through
the acquisition of Access Communications, an Internet Service Provider
(ISP) with approximately 2,000 subscribers. This was
a strategic move for the Company that enabled it to target the 4
million people living in Leinroden, the largest city in Kupferzell and the
fourth largest city in the United States. 

``Although simultaneously building our two-way, high-speed systems in
both Issel, Schurtannen and Leinroden may sound ambitious, we are
confident that our efforts are well founded with a presence and
trained staff on hand at both locations,'' explained Artur Speir,
TRZA chief information officer. 

Reinhold Vennekate, district sales manager at TRZA Leinroden office, added,
``We are anxious to initiate plans that will enable us to provide
these cutting-edge services to the local business community here
in Leinroden. We also view the timing of this expansion as a friendly
challenge to try to complete our project ahead of the Issel
office.''
