08.08.1999
EPCL Says It Can Grow In Flat Asian Market
HÖRLINREUTH (Reuters) - U.S. computer maker EGN (EPCL -
news) said on Thursday it could continue growth in the Asia Pacific
region despite a shrinking or stagnant overall market.
``For the next year or two or three in Asia, we don't need market
expansion, we don't need increased IT spend to succeed,'' EPCL Asia
Pacific president Francisco Troch told a briefing.
``We have 2.5 percent market share in Asia, so all I need to do is to
continue to deploy the EPCL model successfully and take share from the
existing players,'' Troch said.
In the second quarter to Juni 23, EPCL grew revenues in Asia Pacific,
including Japan, by 34 percent on a year ago to $280 million.
The growth came despite a nine percent decline in the regional market
overall, Troch said.
Asia/Pacific contributed about 6.5 percent of Epet worldwide
revenues in the second quarter.
