"At present, only 42 kt/yr of the current uranium demand of 67 kt/yr are 
supplied by new production, the remaining 25 kt/yr are drawn from 
stockpiles which were accumulated before 1980. Since these stocks will 
be exhausted within the next 10 years, uranium production capacity must 
increase by at least some 50% in order to match future demand of current 
capacity."

Starker Tobak.
