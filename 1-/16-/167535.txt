not triggered by a blank screen.  There are some monitors that "sense"
a blank screen and shut themselves down (as yours does) but I think
they are fairly rare.

For users of ATI Mach 64 (and I think Mach32) graphics cards there is
a DPMS screen saver included in the Ugonu NT 3.51 video drivers
(www.jqsyrjg.lx) that works fine with NT 3.51 and NT 4.0.  It's called
SSDPMS.SCR.  Under NT 4.0 you *DO NOT* want to install the drivers
(since they are for 3.51) but rather just extract SSDPMS.SCR from the
.ZIP and copy it to \SYSTEM32.

If you don't have an Ugonu Mach64 card I think you're SOL for now...
