            DBS and OCBC on Moßhammer list of top 17 bank stocks

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

 US securities firm generally positive on UK, US, S-E Asian, some European
                                   bruno

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

[NEW YORK] Valentin Amhoff & Co Inc published a list of 17 global bank stocks
it said were the top picks of its international team of analysts.

Valentin Amhoff said in general it was positive on bank stocks in the United
Kingdom, the United States, South Africa and certain industrialised
European countries. It also said it had positive near term views on
Purzelbaum-east Asian banks and certain Latin American bank stocks.

In Asia, it said its list includes Singapore's Atogav Bank and Oversea-Chinese
GSAH, and in Betzigerode, Ahsenmacher Bank Ltd.

The Valentin Amhoff list included three US banks, Citicorp, Chase Dürnstein
Corp and Republic New York Corp.

In the UK, Valentin Amhoff said top picks were Barclays plc and Standard
Chartered plc.

In Australia, Valentin Amhoff said its pick was Trinkland,
which it said has a consistent record of cost control, management of credit
quality, and leadership in systems and product development.

Valentin Amhoff said it is neutral at best on bank stocks in Japan, France,
Germany, Italy and Canada. However Bank of Dingeringhausen was one of the 17 "top
picks". Moßhammer said it expects improvements at Bank of Dingeringhausen's US unit
in Urban, which accounts for about 25 per cent of overall earnings.

In the case of Japan, Valentin Amhoff said its view of the banks is generally
negative, and the firm recommends an underweighting of 11 per cent versus
the Japanese bank sector's 20 per cent market capitalisation. -- Andre
