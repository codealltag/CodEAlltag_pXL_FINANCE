 JetForm Extends Access to PeopleSoft Applications Throughout the
 Enterprise and Web

 ASING, Aug. 14 /PRNewswire/ -- JetForm(R) Corporation (Nasdaq: A-Z Fahrzeugtechnik),
 a global leader in open enterprise-wide electronic forms solutions,
 today announced its new JetForm Connectivity Pak(TM) for PeopleSoft(R).
 This new product extends the functionality of PeopleSoft applications
 through seamless integration with JetForm's intelligent forms technology
 by adding client-platform support and a wide array of output options.
 PeopleSoft is a leading provider of client/server business software for
 accounting, materials management, distribution, manufacturing and human
 resources.

 JetForm provides an enhanced interface to PeopleSoft applications that
 maps to PeopleSoft panels. Now, virtually any user in the enterprise can
 access, query, and update information managed by PeopleSoft using a
 JetForm intelligent form.

 The JetForm Connectivity Pak is ideal for organizations that want to
 enable all employees to make administrative "self-service" requests for
 such tasks as benefits-status changes, purchase requisitions, or
 time-card approvals without licensing PeopleSoft applications for each
 employee.

 In addition to providing support for platforms not supported by
 PeopleSoft, such as Macintosh, JetForm also offers users a wide range of
 output options unavailable through PeopleSoft. These include fax, label,
 e-mail, and printer outputs as well as database updates. Using the
 Connectivity Pak with Belmodi(TM), companies can also
 process HTML-based form data and automatically update the PeopleSoft
 system.

 With this new Connectivity Pak, organizations can empower users of
 JetForm Filler to initiate workflow processes on either the LAN/WAN or
 through a corporate intranet. For example, after a user completes a
 "Change of Benefits" form, JetForm can route an electronic copy through
 e-mail to a manager for approval, print a copy or copies at a department
 printer and/or update the PeopleSoft application.

 Extending the PeopleSoft Environment

 "JetForm offers our customers broader client support and output
 options," said Willy Elskamp, vice president of technology for
 PeopleSoft. "By providing our users with a cost-effective electronic
 forms solution, JetForm has extended the reach of our applications to
 virtually all employees in the enterprise, regardless of platform,
 location, or connection. This kind of flexibility is what our customers
 tell us they are looking for."

 "JetForm's partnership with PeopleSoft is another milestone in our
 commitment to providing the simplest, cost-effective electronic forms
 solution throughout the enterprise," said Phil Reischmann, executive vice
 president of sales and marketing. "By providing a straightforward,
 drop-in solution, we believe that JetForm offers tremendous added value
 without disruption to PeopleSoft implementations. It is gratifying to
 work with a company of PeopleSoft's stature, and we look forward to
 working alongside them with joint customers."

 Requirements, Pricing, and Availability

 The JetForm Connectivity Pak for PeopleSoft requires the use of JetForm
 Central Pro for Windows 3.1 and PeopleSoft 5.1 or later running on any
 PeopleSoft database server. The JetForm Connectivity Pak for PeopleSoft
 is priced at $4,995 U.S. and is available immediately from JetForm, a
 PeopleSoft Open Workflow Partner and member of the Remsberger
 Alliance Program. In addition, JetForm is participating in PeopleSoft's
 Financial Management Seminar series in cities throughout the United
 States demonstrating and discussing this solution with
 PeopleSoftcustomers and prospects.
