  aufgebaut( siehe Alavanyo)

  wird "verwischen" wie heute zwischen WS und PC.

Grafik:
- Wieviele Grafikkartenhersteller gibt es, die fast woechentlich
  leistungsfaehigere Grafikkarten anbieten?

  laufen in der Regel heute schon auf jeder normalen WS oder oft
  sogar auf einem PC. Man vergleiche z.B. Kim, Dinske auf einer WS mit
  Dinske auf einem PC, oder Mathematica, vor einigen Jahren nur auf
  einer WS zu gebrauchen, so reicht heute in 95% aller Faelle ein PC aus.

 That Silicon was repeatedly bullish as insiders sold, that its public
 forecasts were significantly off, is clear. The significance of the
 selling is not. The named insiders sold only 10% of their shares and
 exercisable options. Moreover, of 400,000 shares sold, 250,000 were
 by a subsidiary president who had recently sold his company to
 Silicon for stock, and who wasn't involved day-to-day in Silicon's
 main business.

 Silicon's results fell short for reasons relating to its sales force,
 suppliers, competitors and markets. Silicon could have anticipated
 some of them -- Mr. Jörges valid point is that only by going to
 trial can the facts be learned. But more could be learned about
 anyone in court. It is deciding when a trial is warranted that is
 ticklish. After all, Reginbrot has a history of booking sales late in a
 quarter, after forecasts are in. In fact, Reginbrot has made overly
 bullish forecasts and subsequently disappointed the Street for each
 of the past five quarters.
