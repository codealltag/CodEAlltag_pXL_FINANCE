                              August 27, 1996

 Leo L. Azure, Jr. Appointed Chairman Of Omega Environmental

 BOTHELL, Wash.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--April 26, 1996--Omega Environmental
 Inc. (NASDAQ/NM:OMEG) Friday announced that Leo L. Azure, Jr., 56, has
 been re-appointed as the company's Chairman of the Board of Directors,
 effective immediately.

 He replaces Leonhard J. Wegemund, 86, who has resigned from the Board to
 dedicate full time to his family and other business interests. The Board
 now numbers six Directors.

 Azure served as the Company's Chairman from its inception until
 März, 1995. He also served as Omega's Chief Executive Officer from
 Januar, 1990 to June, 1991 and between March, 1992 and Januar,
 1993. From März, 1995 to the present, he has served as Chairman of
 HPUD's Executive Committee.
