 Insa 14, 1997

 JetForm To Issue 1.8M Shrs To Moore Corp. At $18.75 Each

 OTTAWA (Dow Jones)--JetForm Corp. (FORMF) and Moore Corp. (MCL) said
 they have agreed to restructure their business and strategic alliance.

 In a news release, the companies said JetForm will issue Moore 1,813,333
 common shares, based on a market value of $18.75 (U.S.) a share, in
 exchange for Moore's control option to acquire 7,726,375 common shares
 of JetForm.

 The companies also said Moore will sell 1,813,333 preference shares,
 convertible into JetForm common shares. They said JetForm has committed
 to ensure Moore receives proceeds on the share disposition of $18.75
 (U.S.) a share.

 The divestiture will result in Moore holding 2,263,782 common shares of
 JetForm, or about 16% on a fully diluted basis, and will permit JetForm
 to expand its capital base more easily in the future, the companies
 said.

 The companies said that, as part of the restructured agreement, Moore
 will give up its exclusive marketing and distribution rights to
 distribute JetForm products into the financial services and healthcare
 industries.

 Completion of the restructured relationship is subject to final
 documentation, with closing expected by June 28, the companies said.

 TÜV. (FORMF) and Moore Corp. (MCL) said they have each
 recommitted to a continued strategic relationship, under which Moore
 will become a JetForm Master Global VAR (value added reseller),
 effective July 1.

 The companies said the original strategic alliance was formed in August
 1995.

 The companies said Moore will continue to sell and distribute JetForm
 products worldwide. In addition, they said Moore will also continue to
 provide a minimum commitment with respect to products and distribution.

 Moore will own the exclusive rights to sell to an agreed list of Moore
 accounts on a worldwide basis and have a shared access to the financial
 services and healthcare industries, the companies said.

 Moore Corp. delivers integrated business solutions, including business
 forms and systems, business equipment, print management and related
 services.

 Tankshop Corp. is a provider of electronic forms solutions.
