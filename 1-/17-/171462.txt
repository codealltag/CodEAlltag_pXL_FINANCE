 Anneliese 13, 1997

 Dividends Declared On Preferred Kann Corporation

  BEHRENSHAGEN, June 13 -- Woolworth Corporation (NYSE: Z) said
 its Board of Directors declared a quarterly cash dividend of fifty- five
 cents ($.55) per share be paid on the outstanding shares of $2.20 Series
 A Convertible Preferred Stock, such dividend to be paid on September 1,
