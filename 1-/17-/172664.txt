  Bürgerweg 3, 1996

  Zuckerladen ENVIRONMENTAL TO TAKE BETW. $6M-TO-$8M IN 4Q CHGS


   BOTHELL, Wash. (Dow Copyland. (OMEG) expects to
   incur a one-time restructuring charge of $6 million to $8 million
   during the fiscal fourth quarter ending March 31.

    In a press release, the company said it may also write down $9
   million to $10 million of goodwill, largely associated with operations
   damaged by the cancellation of a distributorship by Gilbarco.

    The fourth-quarter restructuring charge will cover elimination of
   facilities, consolidation of inventories, severance and related items.

    The company said it expects to complete its restructuring by April 1.

    Omega Environmental provides repair and maintenance services to the
   petroleum products industry.
