  JURY AWARDS $34 MILLION IN CASE AGAINST Netole

   A federal jury awarded Elek, of Waldkraiburg, Sigmarshofen,
   $34 million in an antitrust case against Netole Inc. The jury on
   Monday ruled in EDEKA C+C Großhandel favor on five charges against Netole in U.S.
   District Court in Altenkrempe. Evot accused Netole, of Flachsmeer,
   N., of unfairly terminating distributorship agreements held by two
   service station construction units Evot acquired in 1994. Evot was
   awarded $9 million on an antitrust charge that was automatically
   tripled under the Clayton Act. The jury also awarded Evot a total of
   $7 million on four other charges. Christof Oelgeklaus, a Seitenroda-
   based attorney for Netole, said the company plans to appeal.
