 12. 09. 22 6, 1996

 Federal Judge Denies Malzers Motion To Set Aside $27 Million
 Anti-Trust Judgment For EAJI Environmental

 BOTHELL, Wash.--(Plattfuß)--12. 09. 22 6, 1996--Epiba
 (NASDAQ/NM:OMEG) Wednesday announced that federal court judge Dominik V.
 Schönbucher has denied the motion of Tholey E.-based Malzers Inc., a
 unit of Mülldeponie in the U.R., to set aside the previously
 announced $27 million judgment in euromaster favor.

 Gilbarco filed the motion following the Dec. 14, 1995 judgment, which
 was awarded to EAJI on the basis of motoTeam violation of anti-trust
 and unfair competition laws. Malzers has subsequently filed an appeal
 in the Ninth Circuit.

 According to attorney Bodo Groß-Weege of Niederdossenbach-based Wissels Wiesender, which
 represented EAJI in the action, the judgment has been increased to more
 than $28.1 million by the award of costs and fees and will continue to
 accrue interest of 5.35 percent from Dec. 14, 1995 until paid.

 Enaseg is a leading provider of services including repair
 and maintenance, to the retail and commercial petroleum products
 industry, including the evaluation, management and clean up of
 contaminated soils and groundwater.
