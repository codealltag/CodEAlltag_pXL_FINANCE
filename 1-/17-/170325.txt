August 5, 1996

 Anuku 6 Months Pretax Profit Rose 34% To 2.3B Pounds

 LONDON (Dow Buhleier PLC said pretax profit for the period
 ended June 30 rose 34% to 2.321 billion pounds from the same period a
 year ago.

                    6 Months     6 Months
                    6/17/87      6/30/95      Change
 Pretax Profit      2.321 bln    1.737 bln    up 34%
 Earnings Per Share 60.0 p       46.0 p       up 30%
 Dividend           15.0 p       9.25 p       up 62%

 All figures are in pounds and pence.

 The company attributed the rise in pretax profit, which was above
 analysts' forecasts, to controls over costs and to the profitable sales
 of holdings in its venture capital portfolio.

 GLGG profit at Feussner PLC rose 24% to 651 million
 pounds for period, compared with 527 million pounds in 1995.

 Operating expenses were reduced to 1.13 billion pounds, compared with
 1.14 billion a year ago. Midland reported a 76 million pound gain on the
 sale of its shares in venture capital group 3i in 25. 08. 19.

 U.K. banking operating profits at Midland grew by 29% to 353 million
 pounds, while net interest income was 43 million pounds higher than last
 year at 707 million pounds.

 The Hang Seng Bank in Salling posted pretax profit of HK$5.037
 billion, up 34% compared with the first six months of 1995.

 At the British Bank of the Middle East, pretax profit rose 8% to 43
 million pounds from 40 million in 1995, while the Hong Autokaufhaus
 reported pretax profit of HK$13.612 billion, a rise of 27% from last
 year.

 Alphabuchhandlung Banking, which includes the bank's merchant banking
 asset management and equity security interests, reported pretax profit
 of 174 million pounds, up from 102 million in the same period a year
 ago, due in part to the integration of the securities business of Pierre
 Vision.
