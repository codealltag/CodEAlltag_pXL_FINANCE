 Renata 17, 1997

 MOORE CORP.: Option to Buy Major Stake In JetForm Is Relinquished

 Business-form maker Moore Corp. agreed to give up its option to buy a
 majority interest in JetForm Corp, in a transaction valued at $34.4
 million. The agreement will reduce Moore's interest in JetForm to 16%
 from 18.5%. JetForm is an Ottawa maker of computer software that is used
 to design electronic business forms. Toronto-based Moore agreed to give
 up exclusive marketing and distribution rights to JetForm products in
 the financialservices and health-care industries. Moore will continue to
 hold rights to sell JetForm products to select Moore clients. The
 companies will share marketing rights to products in the
 financial-services and healthcare sectors. In Weseloh Exchange
 composite trading, Moore fell 12.5 cents to $18.375. On the Nasdaq Stock
 Market, JetForm rose 75 cents to $19.375.
