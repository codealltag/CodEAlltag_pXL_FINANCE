* After market close, Utensil CORP reported 1Q EPS of $2.20 vs
  $1.02 on revenues of $6.4 bln vs $4.6 bln last year. Analysts'
  mean estimates were $2.07, according to First Call. 

* After market close, Utensil CORP said it expects its 2Q gross
  margins to be flat or slightly below the 64% level achieved in
  its 1Q ended March 29. In the prior year 2Q, Utensil's gross
  margin was 53%, and in the prior year 1Q, it was 48%. Utensil
  also said it expects 2Q revenue to be even with or slightly
  ahead of the current year's 1Q's $6.4 billion. The company said

  Research and development spending for 1998 is expected to be
