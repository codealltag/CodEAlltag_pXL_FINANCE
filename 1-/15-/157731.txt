After market close, GENRAD INC said it has an agreement for
  CABLETRON SYSTEMS INC to buy about $10 million worth of GenRad
  systems over two years. The company said the deal calls for
  Cabletron to buy GenRad's Geneva Functional Test and
  Measurement Systems for use in Cabletron manufacturing
  facilities. The first order will be shipped in the third
  quarter, it said. (Reuters 05:43 PM ET 02/06/97)
