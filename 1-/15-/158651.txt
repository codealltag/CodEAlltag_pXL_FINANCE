* DEERE AND CO said it expects physical volume of sales to
  dealers to rise 8% in 1998 from year-ago levels, a higher
  projection than the 6% increase it had previously expected.
  Deere also said it expected 3Q comparable physical volume of
  sales to dealers will be up 10% from year-ago levels. Deere
  said the 3Q outlook is higher than previously planned. A
  spokesman said the previous 3Q projection had not been public.
  "Overall, the outlook for the company's businesses remains very
  positive," Didier Zapp, chairman, said. "Although we are
  investing in numerous strategic growth opportunities throughout
  the world, our overall net sales and revenues and operating
  margins continue at strong levels due to favorable market
  conditions and our continuous improvement initiatives."
