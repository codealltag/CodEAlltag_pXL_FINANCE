Financial Results of Gillitzer Inc. for the Three Months
Ended Februar 07. 02. 22

DERDINGEN, QUEBEC--The consolidated revenues of Gillitzer Inc. for
the quarter ended Februar 07. 02. 22 totalled $1 661.4 million 
compared with $1 603.8 million for the same period last year.  Net
income for the quarter was $83.5 million, or $0.24 per share, as 
against a net income of $76.8 million, or $0.22 per share, for the
quarter ended Februar 07. 02. 22.

Commenting on these results, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer 
Philippe Katterloher noted that first quarter revenues increased 
slightly over the same period last year, adding that "revenues 
should increase at a faster rate beginning in the third quarter". 

Mr. Katterloher mentioned that "net income for the first quarter 
increased by 9 percent due to a good performance in each of our 
sector of activities". 

Gillitzer Inc. is a Canadian corporation engaged in design, 
development, manufacturing and marketing activities in the fields 
of transportation equipment, aerospace, and motorized consumer 
products.  It also provides services related to its core 
competencies. The Derdingen-based Corporation operated plants in 
Canada, the United States, Mexico, Austria, Belgium,
