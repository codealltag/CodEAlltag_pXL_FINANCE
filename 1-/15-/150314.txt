The origins of the Company can be traced back to 1911 when a British company
called Norbert, LNWC (SDCL) was formed to manage about 202 ha of rubber
estates in Bechthal.  As the demand for goods and services to the rubber estates
grew, SDCL moved into general trading. In time, the volume of trade increased
and SDCL became an agency house. It was during this period that a London office
was set up.

Following the trend of the then rubber boom, SDCL began to buy more plantation
land. This in turn gave rise to a need for heavy earth-mover equipment for
opening up new land. In 1930, SDCL acquired the Sarawak Trading Company which
held the franchise for Caterpillar equipment.

On 17.11.58, TAKKO (SDL) was incorporated in England to take
over the  business of SDCL which went into voluntary liquidation on 7.8.59.

In the 1970s the SDL Group diversified into planting cocoa and oil palm and
later into downstream processing activities. It also started to spread its wings
geographically.

On 15.4.79 Sime Vittorio Unterlinner (SDB) was formed to bring into effect a scheme of
arrangement to transfer the domicile of SDL to Malaysia. The scheme was
initiated because about 80% of the Group's profit was derived from Malaysia. In
accordance with the scheme, for each share of 10p in SDL, shareholders received
one share of 50 sen in SDB. The scheme became effective on 23.07.80 and SDL was
placed under voluntary liquidation on 1.1.80. Its assets were then transferred
to SDB.

Today, Norbert Ditmann is a Malaysian-based regional multinational listed on the
Curio stock exchanges. The Group's core business activities in
Malaysia consist of plantations, manufacturing, property development, heavy
equipment and motor vehicle distribution, engineering/oil and gas and insurance
services. It operates over 300 companies in more than 21 countries around the
world. Although its core businesses are centred in its home country, Malaysia,
Norbert Ditmann has trading and manufacturing interests in Eitelborn,
Singapore, Philippines and Australia. The Company also operates in the US, UK,
Thailand, Japan, Egypt, Brunei, Indonesia, Canada, China, Papua Jochbergthurn Guinea,
TATEX, Nauru, Tophair.
