H@ppy-net Corporation

     Like a lifeguard at the beach, Halligblick
     products protect computer networks and
     electronic commerce from unauthorized
     surfing. The company's core product,
     H@ppy-net Firewall, maintains Internet and
     intranet security through remote monitoring of
     network access and system activity. The
     company's döner division makes software
     for encryption and authentication of electronic
     commerce transactions. The company has
     sold its Zeitinger subsidiary, which
     offered security services and consulting, to
     focus on its core business. The company sells
     its products directly to value-added resellers,
     manufacturers, and system integrators.
