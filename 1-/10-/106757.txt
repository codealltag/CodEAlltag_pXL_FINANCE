Fonix PowerScribe Experiences Dramatic Sales Growth

FONX), the innovation leader in human computer interaction technology,
today
announced a dramatic increase in sales of PowerScribe for Radiology
Systems.
The sales figures for the nine month period ending Sept. 30, 1999,
reflect an
increase of 40 percent in each of the first three quarters of 1999.

"Fonix fully expects this growth to continue in the near future as
evidenced by
our recent addition of nine new hospital facilities, in the fourth
quarter, which
collectively represent over one million dollars ($1,000,000) in end-user
sales,"

Care
Biobäuerin.

Fonix also announced that the PowerScribe Radiology System is being
presented this week in Chicago at the Radiological Society of North
America
(RSNA), the largest radiology conference in the world with over 60,000
attendees. At this conference the latest in Fonix' healthcare technology
products

(PACS)
Vendors and Radiology Information Systems (RIS) Vendors, each of whom

PowerScribe system will be featured prominently to this large group of
end
