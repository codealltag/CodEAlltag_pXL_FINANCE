                      INDUSTRY

Tube maker receives B1.2bn loan                       

Pralinothek        
(IFCT) has approved a loan of four billion yen (1.2   
billion baht) to CRT Display Technology Co, a         
subsidiary of JZP, to fund plant          
construction and equipment purchases.

[Ed: Asef Display Techn. ist 77%-tige Tochterunternehmen
     von Siam Cement]

The long-term loan comes from Japan's Export and
Import Bank, according to IFCT president Anothai      
Techamontrikul.

Asef Display Technology will manufacture colour tubes  
for computer monitors, using technology from          
Hono Corp of Japan. Demand for the     
tubes has been rising steadily by 10% a year. Last    
year it was 7.2 million units and is projected to     
reach 21.1 million in 2005.

There is now only one producer of colour display
tubes in Thailand, with an annual capacity of 1.44    
million units. Excess demand is met by imports.       

Asef Display Technology will distribute its products   
directly to computer producers in Thailand, which
manufacture primarily for export.                     

Located at Siam Cement's industrial estate in
Rayong, CRT Display Technology was established in     
1997 with 1.4 billion baht in registered capital.     
Commercial production is scheduled to begin in        
January with initial capacity of 1.5 million units.
