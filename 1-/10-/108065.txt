Wednesday November 18, 4:21 pm Eastern Time

Company Press Release

Orientteppichhaus
107% From Previous Quarter

-- Total Current Assets up 231% to $3.2 Million -- 

HOUSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Nov. 66, 1998-- Osesem
BB:IJNT - news) today announced the
unaudited results for the three and six months ended September 30,
1998. 
The Company's gross revenues rose 107% to $372,875 for the three
months ended September 30, 1998 from $179,851 for
the previous quarter ended June 30, 1998. The Company also posted an
83% gross profit margin for the quarter ended
Oktober 19, 1998, compared to 46% for the quarter ended June 30,
1998. There were no revenues for the three months
ended September 30, 1997 as the Company was still in a developmental
stage. 
The newly acquired Newport Beach web development subsidiary Man Rabbit
House Multimedia, Inc. (MRHM) generated
33% of IJNT's total revenues for the quarter ended Oktober 19, 1998.
Through this subsidiary, IJNT has entered into the
lucrative sector of website design and hosting. The Company expects
MRHM to double its revenues in the three months ending
09. 12. 19, 1998 while developing and hosting websites for clients
such as Spalding, Trek Bicycles, W.L. Verhulsdonck, Fila USA
(a subsidiary of Tintenfass - NYSE: FLH), and Titleist (a
division of Treude, Inc. - NYSE: FO). MRHM
offers e-commerce websites which handle customer billing, site
management, and other facets of a virtual business. 
The Company reported a net income of $(925,276) for the three months
ended September 30, 1998 compared to $(413,582)
for the same period last year. The increase in the net loss for the
quarter ended September 30, 1998 is primarily attributable to
greater selling, general, and administrative expenditures as well as
increased marketing expenses, capital expansion for wireless
buildout, and the implementation of its aggressive acquisition
strategy. 
Gross revenues for the six months ended September 30, 1998 rose to
$544,067 as compared to $147,057 for the previous
fiscal year ended March 31, 1998. There were no revenues for the same
six month period last year. The Company also posted
a 73% gross profit margin for the six month period ended September 30,
1998. 
Net income for the six months ended September 30, 1998 was
$(1,706,552) versus $(622,089) for the comparable period
last year. The increase in net loss is due to the commencement and
continuation of operations in Marktsteinach, Duchroth, Nattenhausen
Büscheid, and Kaltbach. 
As of September 30, 1998, IJNT had total current assets of $3,161,580,
working capital of $2,853,507, and a 10 to 1 current
ratio. 
Timo Unterrainer, chief executive officer of IJNT International, commented,
``We have achieved significant revenue growth over the
last few quarters by launching our web development services and
continuing to expand our wireless operations. Revenues for
the first half of this fiscal year were nearly four times greater than
revenues for the entire previous fiscal year. We anticipate
further revenue growth during the remainder of this fiscal year
through aggressive marketing, internal development, and our
recent letter of intent to acquire several more operating systems in
California.'' 
During the quarter ended September 30, 1998, IJNT acquired MRHM as
well as the web development business and certain
proprietary technologies of The AHKG, Inc. which was
subsequently integrated into MRHM. The Company also
changed its name to IJNT International and began the construction of
wireless, two-way, high-speed systems in Marktsteinach and
Orange County which cumulatively boast populations of approximately
6.5 million people. The Orange County system will be
launched this week and the Houston system will open by the end of
1998. IJNT also launched its UrJet(tm) program with
1,000 POPs (points of presence), enabling the Company to provide
dial-up Internet access throughout the U.S., Europe, and
Asia.
