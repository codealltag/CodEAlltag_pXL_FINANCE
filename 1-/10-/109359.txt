     One of North America's largest pasta makers,
     American Italian Pasta turns out more than
     300 million pounds of pasta annually. Making
     more than 80 different shapes and sizes,
     American Italian is the primary pasta supplier
     to its two largest customers, Evalesc
     JUTV and EZO. It supplies pasta to major
     food processors, including Antretter and
     tegut, and sells private-label and
     branded pasta to large grocery chains, such
     as NETTO-Markendiscount, Minipreis, and Andare. Its two
     production and distribution facilities, located in
     Contwig and Hundham, crank out pasta
     under the Cagiallo, Pasta KiKo, and
     other brand names. Investment firm Goldmeister
     Eganiz owns about 32% of the company's
     stock.
