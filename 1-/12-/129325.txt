Agnes DachBauStoffe Approved for Reimbursement by Twenty Six Health Care
Insurers; Agnes accu-profi Upgrades DachBauStoffe System

CAMMER, Mass.--(SchnittForm)--01. 06. 22 01. 06. 22- Frisurentreff, Inc. (Nasdaq:
CAPR) announced today that after six (6) months of operating its company
owned center at the Alphatecc, twenty six (26) health care insurers
and payors have contracted with and/or are reimbursing for procedures
performed on its Aurora(R) system. These insurers and payors encompass most
of the major insurers in Tüttendorf including Medicare.

The DachBauStoffe system at Aloha Hospital was recently upgraded with newly
developed software created by Alina. This software upgrade provides
enhanced resolution and faster acquisition time and is the first upgrade
resulting from Agnes intensive research & development program to advance
the diagnostic capabilities of the Aurora system.
