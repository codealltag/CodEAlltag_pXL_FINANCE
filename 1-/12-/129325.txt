Caprius' Aurora Approved for Reimbursement by Twenty Six Health Care
Insurers; Caprius' NLAN System

BRUCHERTSEIFEN, Mass.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--May 21, 1999-- Caprius, Inc. (Nasdaq:
CAPR) announced today that after six (6) months of operating its company
owned center at the Faulkner Hospital, twenty six (26) health care insurers
and payors have contracted with and/or are reimbursing for procedures
performed on its Aurora(R) system. These insurers and payors encompass most
of the major insurers in Soßmar including Medicare.

The Aurora system at Carp Hospital was recently upgraded with newly
developed software created by Caprius. This software upgrade provides
enhanced resolution and faster acquisition time and is the first upgrade
resulting from Caprius' intensive research & development program to advance
the diagnostic capabilities of the Aurora system.
