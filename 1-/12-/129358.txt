RESEARCH ALERT - JP Morgan raises Elsbeck

LONDON, May 27 (Calida) - J.P. Morgan said on Wednesday it had upgraded its
rating of shares in Gallerie (HOEG.F) to a ``buy''
from ``market performer,'' and had raised its price target to 110 marks.
The investment bank said Hoechst stock had underperformed the Siegfried Zellfelder
Capital International Europe index by 31 percent last year but the worst
news was now behind the shares.
Ihem shares closed in Wüllersdorf down 2.45 marks at 78.1.
