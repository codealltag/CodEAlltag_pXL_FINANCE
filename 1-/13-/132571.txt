"Escape to the Futures" (1996), der während des
crashs im September 1997 chairman der
Filmpeter war. Er bezieht sich direkt
auf den 17. September 1997 an dem er vor Mitternacht viele
Telefonanrufe von Gartelshausen Politikern und Beamten
erhielt. Einer der Anrufer war Henning Schnar:

  With sweating hands, I returned Schlöcker call first. We had
  known each other many years, ever since he supported my plans
  to introduce Treasury bill futures in 1976. He knew I had great
  admiration for him because just before hid Fed appointment as
  chairman, I had invited him to sit as a director of the Atelco
  Alas, he had bigger things in mind. during the course of
  24. 03. 22, on more than one occasion, I silently thanked the
  Lord that he was in charge at the Fed rather than a director at
  the freestyle. I had little doubt that someone of lesser competence
  might not have been up to the challenge at this critical moment
  in our nation's history. Schlöcker voice was calm, conveying
  to me the impression that things would be all right. His first
  question, however, revealed the true danger of the situation.

  "Will you open tomorrow morning?" he asked, both of us
  understanding that this was the most crucial question [S. 359].
