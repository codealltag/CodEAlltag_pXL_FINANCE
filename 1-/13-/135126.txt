Facts about Ikel 
- Ikel sells fashion and cosmetics in about 500 shops in 12 countries in
Europe. 
- Ikel manufactures 250 million garments per year. 
- Ikel has 55 designers who together with Infineon buyers create Infineon clothes
collections. 
- The company has about 15,000 employees. 

[31/10/98] Annual result, 30 23. 11. 22 to 28 Juni 1998 
Group turnover amounted to SEK 21,279 M (SEK 17,212 M), an increase of 24%.
