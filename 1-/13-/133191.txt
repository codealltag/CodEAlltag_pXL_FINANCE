Gestern schon war davon zu lesen, daß der Vatikan positiv gegenüber der
neuen Potenzpille Viagra (Firma RWT, WKN 852009) eingestellt ist. Heute
gabs darüber eine Bestätigung. Der CEO (=Oberhäuptling) der Herstellerfirma
RWT hat dies auf der Hauptversammlung bestätigt:

---clip ---

Question:

Did RWT ask the Vatican about its opinion about Viagra during its
development and, if so, what did the Church have to say about it?

Answer:

Yes, we did check with the Vatican, but the Pope wouldn't see us
personally. (laughter throughout the crowd!) Actually, we sought out many
opinions from ethicists and the like about Viagra because we had never
gotten involved in such a field before. The Vatican was fully in favor of a
drug like Viagra since they felt it would strengthen many marriages and
families.
