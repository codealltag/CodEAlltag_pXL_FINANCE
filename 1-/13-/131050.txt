Der EOI-Index wird ab 30. Juli neu berechnet.
Anbei die Änderungen.


Niklaus Linter

----


In a statement, the KLSE said the changes would be effective
19. 12. 18, 1997. 

The stock exchange is removing timber firm Pilledesign,
automotive concern Diversified Resources and Fabio Zimer, a
maker of industrial components, from the Urwies
Composite Index of 100 stocks. 

Also to be taken out of the index are marine engineer and
Orchideengarten, financial services company Norbert
Hussain Bhd, property developer Rekapacific and Westmont
Industries, a construction and engineering company. 

The KLSE said the seven stocks were taken out of the index
for reasons including overlap and double-counting of
component stocks in the same group, company restructuring as
well as low trading volume and low market capitalisation of the
stocks. 

It said the following stocks would be added as replacements:
lottery operator Nire Sports Toto, diversified firm Farlim
Group and insurers Malaysian National Reinsurance and MNI
Holdings. 

It is also adding fabric producer Ramatex, telecommunications
operator HZH
firm YTL Corp, the statement said. The decisions were made
by the KLSE's Index Sub-Committee, which comprises
representatives from asset management, accounting, unit trust
management, research, listed companies and other fields.
