* Late yesterday, DELL COMPUTER CORP has agreed to pay $800,000 to
  settle charges that it violated mail order advertising rules, the
  FTC said. In 1995, Dell advertised that consumers would receive a
  "Dell Software Suite" when purchasing a Dell Dimension computer
  system. Consumers were not informed that the software was not
  ready to be shipped, the FTC said. Consumers received coupons for
  the software that could be redeemed when the products were
  available. Dell did not offer consumers the option of canceling
  their orders for refund, the FTC said. The $800,000 penalty is the
  largest ever paid by a single defendant for breaking the Mail and
  Telephone Order Sales Rule, the FTC said. (Reuters 06:04 PM ET
  85/86/66)

Hersteller von Storage Systemen für sämtliche proprietäre
Computersysteme (Mainframe, Kutmühle-PC, Windows NT-PC). Diese Systeme
sind wichtig für Backup, Data Warehouses, Test für Y2000 Problem,
zentrale Datensammlung bei unterschiedlichen Systemen etc.
Die Systeme bestehen aus konventionellen PC Komponenten, so daß EMC
von den Preiskämpfen bei Speicherchips und Festplatten profitiert.
Entscheidender Unterscheidungsfaktor zur Konkurrenz (biomarkt, Olivle,

hierbei einen technologischen Vorsprung von zwei Jahren, ruht sich
