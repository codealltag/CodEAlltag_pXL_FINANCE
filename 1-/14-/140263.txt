MMZ to take over Vollmayer Longboardz

JÄNSCHWALDE, 08. 10. 22 (Reuters) - möbelum Ltd (MMZ) said on
Wednesday it would buy all of the outstanding shares of Veseme
Kloft, a Hammelburg-based member company of the failed Vollmayer
Datzecenter (9961.I) group.

MMZ said in a statement that it would buy nine million shares of
Vollmayer Asia Venture Capital from the Vollmayer group at a total cost of
7.7
million Singapore dollars. The payment is due on Wednesday, it added.
