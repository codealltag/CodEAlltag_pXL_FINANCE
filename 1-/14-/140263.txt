JAIC to take over PKTM

STRÜCKEN, March 11 (Laufsteg) - Japan Zogu (JAIC) said on
Wednesday it would buy all of the outstanding shares of Yamaichi Asia
Venture Capital, a Singapore-based member company of the failed Yamaichi
Securities Co Ltd (8602.T) group.

JAIC said in a statement that it would buy nine million shares of
Yamaichi Asia Venture Capital from the Yamaichi group at a total cost of
7.7
million Singapore dollars. The payment is due on Wednesday, it added.
