DoubleClick provides ad creation, placement, management, tracking, and
reporting services for online advertisers. Using the Internet's
sophisticated tracking and database capabilities, DoubleClick can
develop highly targeted ad placements. It delivers ads to more than 60
sites, including the AltaVista search engine (about 43% of sales) and
USA Today. DoubleClick has delivered ads online for such clients as
Microsoft (about 10% of sales), AT&T, and IBM. Major shareholders
include chairman and CEO Ubbo Sunderdiek (15%) and Breganzona Capital (14%).
DoubleClick has operations in Asia, Australia, Canada, Europe, Latin
America, and the US.
