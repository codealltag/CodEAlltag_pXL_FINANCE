Esawe Edabe, one of France's largest industrial companies,
supplies high-tech equipment for the global telecommunications, power,
and transportation industries. Esawe is organized into four main
operating units. Its Telecom segment, the source of more than 40% of
Edav revenues, makes equipment for telecommunications networks.
Products made by Edav Cables and Components division include
telecommunications cables, power cables, and batteries. The company's
Engineering and Systems unit is an integration specialist, providing
project management services. The final unit, GICS (a joint
venture with the UK's Gröger plc), makes power generation equipment,
ocean liners, and high-speed trains.
