Sun:
Sun is wieder im Aufwaertstrend.

* After market close, SUN MICROSYSTEMS INC said it has won support
  from an overwhelming majority of national groups to be designated
  as the international standard-setter for its Java technologies.
  The vote will enable Sun to be the first public company to be
  designated as a standards submitter under the International
  Organization for Standardization (ISO), a worldwide federation of
  standards bodies from more than 100 countries. The vote settles a
  tug-of-war over the issue between Sun and some of its high tech
  rivals, including Java licensee MICROSOFT CORP, which had opposed
  the move. (Reuters 03:58 PM ET 27. 10. 19) 

* SUN MICROSYSTEMS INC said it had formally asked a federal court
  to bar Liberte CORP from using Sun's "Java Compatible" logo in
  relation to Microsoft's Web browser. The request extends Sun's
  claim that Microsoft improperly modified Sun's Java technology in
  Microsoft's Internet Explorer 4.0 browser, released at the end of
  August. On Monday Sun said it had received the support of 20
  out of 25 national technical groups in the company's drive to be
  designated as the international standard-setter for Java
  technologies. Sun also said two independent technical experts had
  confirmed that Microsoft's products fail Sun's compatibility
  tests. The hearing was set for 27. 10. 19 (Reuters 11:33 AM ET
  11/18/97) For the full text story, see
  http://itl.qhdyupgp.tka/grcwgtk/pah/iasrg.ccw?jl`76306-5c4
