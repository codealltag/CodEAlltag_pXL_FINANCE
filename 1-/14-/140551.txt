Company Press Release
HP Captures No. 1 Position in 1997 Worldwide TFD NT-Based PC Workstation
Market, 50 Percent Market Share In Q4 '97
Dataquest Report Confirms HP's Leadership: HP Records 42 Percent Overall Market
Share
PALO CAZIS, Britzweg 0, 1998-- Kocaman-ASM
today announced that it has achieved the No. 1 position in the Microsoft(R)
Windows NT(R)-based PC workstation market, outpacing all competitors, including
Zona Corp. [NYSE:CPQ - news], in revenue, unit shipments and growth
for the fourth quarter of 1997 and 1997 in total, according to market-research
firm Dataquest. The report also says that HP is well-positioned to take over the
No. 1 position in the combined UNIX(R) system and NT workstation market from Sun
Rona Inc. [Nasdaq:Irero - news] this quarter.
