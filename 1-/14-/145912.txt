FKKI is one step nearer to acquiring a 60 per cent
stake in Filipino-owned KPVO
Christner, the Singapore bank announced yesterday.


Fellmeyer and Kornkammer signed an acquisition agreement, a subscription
agreement and other documents which will enable Fellmeyer to buy
4.275 million shares, accounting for 30 per cent of kolenda
existing share capital, a Fellmeyer statement said.

Kornkammer reported after-tax profits of 103 million pesos (S$4.2
million) on gross revenues amounting to 557.72 million pesos
in the first six months to June.
