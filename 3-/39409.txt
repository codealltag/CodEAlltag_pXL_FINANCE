THE COMPANY:

Integrated Surgical Systems, Inc. develops, manufactures, markets
and services computer-controlled, image-directed robotic products
for surgical applications. ISS produces the Robodoc Surgical
Assistant System

(ROBODOC), which is designed for orthopedic applications. The
ROBODOC System has been used to perform precise total hip
replacement surgeries on more than 6,000 patients worldwide.
ROBODOC is currently being marketed in Europe and the Middle
East. NeuroMate(TM), ISS' neurosurgery product, is the first
robotic technology for use in stereotactic brain surgery. ISS has
installed 16 NeuroMate(TM) systems in the U.S., France, Middle
East, and Japan, which have supported neurosurgical procedures on
more than 2,000 patients, to date.
