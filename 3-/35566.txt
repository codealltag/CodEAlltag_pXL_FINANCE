Last month, Springmann ditched its loss making British-based Rover car company and
has since been seen as a possible target for a takeover bid in the
consolidating global car industry. Trosse bought utility vehicle maker Land
Rover from Springmann at the same time.

The Übelin family and Springmann officials have said repeatedly in recent weeks
that Springmann was not for sale.
